What I Treat

How Can I Help You?

Click a condition to learn more about how physiotherapy can help.

🦴

Back & Neck Pain

One of the most common reasons people seek physiotherapy. Whether it's a sudden acute episode or long-standing chronic pain, a structured programme of hands-on therapy, targeted exercise, and postural advice can dramatically reduce pain and prevent recurrence.

See Full Service →
🏃

Sports Injuries

From sprains and strains to tendinopathies and stress fractures — I work with athletes of all levels to get you back performing safely and stronger than before. Your return-to-sport timeline is built around your body, not a generic protocol.

See Full Service →
🔄

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Surgery is only half the recovery. Structured rehabilitation after knee replacements, ACL reconstruction, hip surgery, or spinal procedures is critical to regaining full function. I work closely with your surgical team to follow evidence-based protocols.

See Full Service →
💪

Shoulder Problems

Rotator cuff injuries, frozen shoulder, impingement, and instability are all conditions I treat regularly. Accurate diagnosis is key — I'll identify the exact structure involved and create a plan to restore full, pain-free movement.

See Full Service →
🦵

Knee Pain

Whether it's runner's knee, ligament sprains, meniscal issues, or osteoarthritis — knee pain responds well to physiotherapy. I'll assess the whole kinetic chain, not just the knee, to address the root cause and keep it from coming back.

See Full Service →
🧠

Chronic Pain

Persistent pain is complex, but manageable. Using a biopsychosocial approach, I help patients understand their pain, build confidence in movement, and gradually improve quality of life — without relying on ongoing passive treatment.

See Full Service →
📐

Posture & Movement

Poor movement patterns and posture often underlie many pain conditions. A comprehensive movement assessment identifies imbalances and areas of weakness, allowing us to correct the root cause before it becomes a bigger problem.

See Full Service →

About Me

A Physiotherapist Who Listens First

I'm Tiago, a qualified and HCPC-registered physiotherapist with over five years of clinical experience treating a wide range of musculoskeletal and sports-related conditions.

My approach is evidence-based and patient-centred — I take the time to understand your goals, lifestyle, and history before building a treatment plan that genuinely works for you, not a textbook version of your condition.
